Who is Ganesha?

Origin

Ganesha, also commonly known as Ganapati or Vinayaka, is a prominent god in the Hindi religion. He is known as the remover of obstacles, patron of wisdom, and thought to bring good luck.

He's famous for his tusked elephant head and is commonly depicted with four arms and a protruding belly.
